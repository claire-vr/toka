first-time setup
This app uses Google Sheets as its database and Google Drive for receipts — both free and in your Google account.

1 — Open Google Sheets, create a blank spreadsheet named toka-expenses.

2 — Click Extensions → Apps Script. Delete existing code, paste toka-expenses-backend.gs, save.

3 — Click Deploy → New deployment → Web app → Anyone → Deploy. Copy the URL.

4 — Paste it below. Share the same URL with your team.
